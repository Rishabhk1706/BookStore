How To Use

1. Signup/Login

Create an account or login from the top-right side of the navbar. After login your name appears with a welcome message.

2. Browse Books

Open the Books page and use search, category filters, sorting, and pagination to find books easily.

3. Add to Cart / Wishlist

Open any book detail page and decide whether to purchase now or save it for later.

4. Place Orders

From Cart, review subtotal, tax, and final total, then place your order and track it on the Orders page.
